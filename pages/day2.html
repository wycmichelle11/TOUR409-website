Day 2 - Universal Studios and Sunset River Dinner Cruise
  • Home
  • Day 2

If you travel to Singapore, how could you not drop by Universal Studios Singapore (USS)?

We understand the pain of lining up for rides on a Sunday afternoon. That is why our tickets are not only discounted, but also express, allowing you to skip the lines to experience Universal Studios Singapore all in one day.

See what's different in USS, and have a full day of fun! A promo code will be given after purchase of the package. Buy now to get your tickets!

We know you had a long two days. First with Comic Con and then USS. Wrapping up your second day, we hope you can relax on a river cruise to look around central Singapore, and take in the beauty it has to bring. We have chosen the perfect time at sunset and dinner time, to view the sun winding down as you enjoy your dinner.

We have partnered with Singapore River Cruise to bring you around Boat Quay, Clarke Quay and Marina Bay. As a bonus to our package, there will be an exclusive opportunity to stay on the boat to watch the Marina Bay Sands Spectra Light and Water Show up close. You won't get this anywhere else!

The Light and Water Show tells a story through fountain jets, visual projections, mist, and laser effects. Accompanied by the sound, it represents the majestic Singapore.

The dinner cruise will last around 2 hours and an extra hour for the MBS water show.

What are you waiting for? Buy now!
